# Sabor & Harmonia

**Sabor & Harmonia** is a fictional restaurant website created as an academic project, with a focus on dietary inclusion. The concept is to present a restaurant that respects and embraces different types of diets â€” from omnivorous to restrictive â€” whether due to health conditions, ethical choices, or lifestyle preferences.

## Objective

The goal of this project is to demonstrate, through front-end development, how a restaurant can communicate clearly, accessibly, and inclusively with its audience, offering a pleasant and informative user experience.
